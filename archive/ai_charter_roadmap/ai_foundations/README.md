# AI Foundations Breakout

**Session:** AI Foundations Breakout (Wednesday)
**Format:** Slides (15 min) + pre-recorded vibe coding demo (~30 min)
**Audience:** Commercial team (opt-in)

---

## The Focus

AI 101 for non-technical team members. Meet people where they are. Then show them what's possible with a vibe coding demo.

Ties into Shiv's main stage talk about AI tools at Abridge.

---

## Part 1: Foundations (~15 min)

- LLMs and how they work (high level)
- Generative vs agentic AI
- Prompting basics (Role/Context/Task/Format framework)
- Tool landscape (Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity)

## Part 2: Vibe Coding Demo (~30 min)

- Poll audience: "What would make your life easier?" — collect ideas/sketches
- Pick one feasible idea (pre-select options that are doable)
- Pre-recorded demo of building a first pass
- Show that this stuff is accessible and fast
- "People can present ideas and sketches of things they'd want"
